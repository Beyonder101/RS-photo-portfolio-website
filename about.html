Rohan Harnal, a seasoned Delhi-based photographer with nearly twelve years of experience, captures life's essence through his lens. He pursued his education in photojournalism at Aurobindo College and studio photography at SPEOS in Paris, his work ranges from Delhi's vibrant streets to Paris' contemporary studios.

He has worked with clients like Rani Pink Gifts, Sepoy & Co., Simba Beer, and DIVA Restaurants. His expertise extends to commercial fashion and celebrity portraiture, with a notable engagement at Women’s Era magazine for their May 2018 issue, his work highlighted the unique beauty and personality of each individual.

For Rohan, creative photography resonates with the target audience and inspires engagement is more than making things look pretty, but it also resonates with the target audience and inspires them to engage with the business.
